Colonialismo

Esplorazione delle potenze coloniali europee

In this website, we aim to explain the phenomenon of colonialism, a historical and political process through which a state extends its control over territories and populations beyond its own borders . This process, which developed with particular intensity between the 15th and 20th centuries, was primarily driven by the search for resources, new markets, and international prestige.
Colonial powers often imposed their authority through force, redefining borders, exploiting natural resources, and enforcing cultural, economic, and social models that were foreign to the local populations. The consequences of colonialism have been profound and long-lasting: they influenced the economic development, political structures, and cultural identities of the colonized countries, contributing to inequalities and tensions that still persist today.

The website illustrates the phenomenon of colonialism over the period between 1500 and 2000. Thanks to an interactive timeline, users can thoroughly explore the historical evolution of colonialism and analyze the correlations between major colonial events and their temporal context. In this way, the user is able to visually grasp the moments of peaks and declines in colonization.

Each static bubble represents a colonialist nation

while the abstract dots represent the colonized countries, whose size is linked to the duration of their colonization.
